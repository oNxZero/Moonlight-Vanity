Mouse click sounds sourced from [Keyboard Sounds Pro](https://github.com/keyboard-sounds/keyboardsounds-pro) bundled profiles (MIT License).

Profiles included:
- Razer Mamba Elite
- Logitech G502 Lightspeed
- Logitech G502 X Wireless
- Corsair M65 Elite
- Apple Magic Mouse
- Amazon Gaming Mouse
- Logitech M570 Trackball

Default uses the Razer Mamba Elite left-click sample.
